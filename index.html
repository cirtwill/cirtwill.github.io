I am a PhD student currently working with Dr. Daniel Stouffer at the University of Canterbury, Christchurch, New Zealand. My primary focus is understanding how species' traits (parasite vs. free-living, phylogeny, etc.) affect how they fit into food webs and other ecological networks (e.g., plant-pollinator networks). I've also worked on macroecological patterns in food-web structure and connecting trophic interactions to island biogeography.

I completed my BSc working with Dr. Tamara Romanuk in 2012.

Please check out my CV and papers, and feel free to contact me if you'd like to know more about my work.
